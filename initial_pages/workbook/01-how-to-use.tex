\wbPageHeader{How to Use This Workbook}{Four steps to get the most from every section.}

% ── Diamond Steps ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\begin{center}
\begin{minipage}{0.84\textwidth}

\wbDiamondStep{1}{funBlue}{Preview the Concept}{%
    Each section opens with a short reminder of the key idea or formula.
    If something feels unfamiliar, pair this workbook with the study
    guide for a deeper explanation before jumping into problems.}

\wbDiamondStep{2}{funTeal}{Work the Problems in Order}{%
    Problems are arranged from basic to challenging. Starting with
    the easier ones builds confidence and warms up your thinking
    before the harder questions arrive.}

\wbDiamondStep{3}{funOrange}{Show Every Step}{%
    Write out every step on scratch paper. If you get a wrong answer,
    your written work helps you pinpoint exactly where things went
    off track --- so you can fix it and learn.}

\wbDiamondStep{4}{funPurple}{Review and Correct}{%
    After finishing a section, flip to the Answer Key and check every
    problem. For each mistake, figure out \emph{why} it happened.
    Then try a similar problem again. That's how real learning works.}

\end{minipage}
\end{center}
