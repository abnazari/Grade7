]
    \small\color{funGrayDark}
    Seven tests create the ideal forging arc: warm-up, strength-building,
    and final tempering. Each heat adds resilience.

    \medskip
    \textbf{Support your apprentice by:}
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=1pt]
        \item Spacing tests 2--3 days apart for practice and review
        \item Reviewing wrong answers together without judgment --- every mistake
            is part of the forging process
        \item Celebrating improvement in technique (showing steps, labeling units)
            not just raw scores
        \item Keeping sessions to 45--60 minutes to maintain focus
    \end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
