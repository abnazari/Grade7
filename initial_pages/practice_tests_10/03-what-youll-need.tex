    \small\color{funGrayDark}
    Ten practice tests provide the most comprehensive preparation available.
    The 4-phase structure (Foundation $\to$ Framework $\to$ Finishing $\to$ Capstone)
    ensures skills build progressively and durably.

    \medskip
    \textbf{Keys to a successful build:}
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=1pt]
        \item Space tests 2--3 days apart --- never more than one per day
        \item Phases I--II: review answers together and discuss strategies
        \item Phases III--IV: let them work independently, then debrief results
        \item Compare Test 1 with Test 10 to celebrate the full construction arc
    \end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
