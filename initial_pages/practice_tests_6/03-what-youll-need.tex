    \small\color{navDeep}
    Six voyages offer a focused yet thorough preparation.
    The 3-phase structure (Coastal $\to$ Open Sea $\to$ Charted Mastery)
    builds confidence and skill progressively.

    \medskip
    \textbf{Keys to a successful voyage:}
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=1pt]
        \item Space tests 2--3 days apart --- never more than one per day
        \item Voyages I--II: review answers together and discuss strategies
        \item Voyage III: let them navigate alone, then discuss results
        \item Compare Test~1 with Test~6 to celebrate the entire journey
    \end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
