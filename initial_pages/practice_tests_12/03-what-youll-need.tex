    \small\color{musBlack}
    Twelve practice tests provide the most comprehensive concert series available.
    The 4-movement structure (Prelude $\to$ Development $\to$ Intermezzo $\to$ Finale)
    ensures mastery builds progressively and durably.

    \medskip
    \textbf{Keys to a standing ovation:}
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=1pt]
        \item Space tests 2--3 days apart --- never more than one per day
        \item Prelude \& Development: review answers together and discuss
        \item Intermezzo: let them rehearse independently, then review results
        \item Finale: fully independent; compare Test~1 with Test~12 for the full symphony
    \end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
