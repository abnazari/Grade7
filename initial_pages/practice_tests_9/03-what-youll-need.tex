    \small\color{alpSlate}
    Nine tests provide the most thorough preparation path available.
    The 3-phase structure (Base Camp $\to$ Ridge $\to$ Summit Push)
    builds skills progressively and sustainably.

    \medskip
    \textbf{Keys to a successful ascent:}
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=1pt]
        \item Space tests 2--3 days apart --- never more than one per day
        \item Base Camp: review answers together and discuss strategies
        \item The Ridge: work independently, then debrief weak areas
        \item Summit Push: full independence; compare Test~1 with Test~9
    \end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
