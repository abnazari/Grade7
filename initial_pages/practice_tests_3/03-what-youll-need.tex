    \medskip
    \textbf{How to support the launch:}
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=1pt]
        \item Let Mission 1 be \textit{untimed} --- it's about discovering strengths and gaps.
        \item After each mission, review the answer key together. Ask: \textit{``What strategy could you try next time?''}
        \item Use the Mission Log to celebrate tangible progress.
        \item If specific topics keep showing up, pair this book with our
            \textbf{Grade 7 Math Study Guide} for targeted review.
    \end{itemize}
