Not all mistakes are equal. Sort each wrong answer into one of these three categories:

\vspace{3mm}

\begin{center}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.5}
\begin{tabular}{|>{\bfseries\color{funOrangeDark}\centering\arraybackslash}p{2.3cm}|p{4.2cm}|>{\centering\arraybackslash}p{2.2cm}|}
\hline
\rowcolor{funOrange!15} \textbf{Type} & \textbf{What Happened} & \textbf{How Many?} \\
\hline
\faTimesCircle~Careless & Knew it but messed up (sign error, calculation slip) & \rule[-2pt]{1.2cm}{0.5pt} \\
\hline
\faQuestion~Concept & Didn't know the method or formula & \rule[-2pt]{1.2cm}{0.5pt} \\
\hline
\faEyeSlash~Misread & Misunderstood what the question asked & \rule[-2pt]{1.2cm}{0.5pt} \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
\end{tcolorbox}

\vspace{3mm}

{\bfseries\color{funYellowDark} \faLightbulb\hspace{2mm}Each Type Has a Different Fix}

\vspace{2mm}

\textbf{Careless} $\rightarrow$ Slow down, show work, double-check. \textbf{Concept} $\rightarrow$ Study that topic and re-read explanations. \textbf{Misread} $\rightarrow$ Underline key words before solving. Keep tracking this — you'll see the pattern shift over time!
\end{tcolorbox}
