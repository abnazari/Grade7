\vspace{2mm}

Doing math in your head feels fast, but it's where most careless mistakes hide. When you write out your steps:

\vspace{3mm}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=6mm, itemsep=3pt]
    \item[\textcolor{funTeal}{\faCheck}] You can see exactly where you went wrong if the answer doesn't match
    \item[\textcolor{funTeal}{\faCheck}] You catch sign errors, decimal mistakes, and missing steps
    \item[\textcolor{funTeal}{\faCheck}] Your brain stays organized instead of juggling numbers
    \item[\textcolor{funTeal}{\faCheck}] You can check your work faster at the end
\end{itemize}

\vspace{2mm}

Think of scratch paper as your sidekick — it makes you stronger, not slower.
\end{tcolorbox}
